About POTW

The Problem of The Week is a weekly programming problem designed to test topics in competitive programming such as dynamic programming, greedy algorithms, binary search, etc. These problems vary in difficulty and are designed to test your programming knowledge and help you learn as well.

Our problems are held in seasons of 10 problems each. We are currently in Season 3! A new POTW gets released every week on Sunday at 12:01 AM (EST) and ends on Saturday at 11:59 PM. We use HackerRank to display and grade the problems. You can submit your solution with any language. All problems will be accessible at the corresponding HackerRank contest at any time. You can access the problems via our Problem Archive. Below is the link to the current season's HackerRank contest.
