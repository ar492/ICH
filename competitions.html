Labor Day 2020

About

There are 5 problems: all of them are high quality, and great opportunities to learn from. The problems are arranged in ascending order of difficulty (USACO Bronze->Gold) or (CodeForces 900->1700).

Rules and Scoring

There are 15 test-cases for each problem, and each problem is worth 15 points (1 pt per test-case). Ties are be broken by the sum of the times for the first correct submission for each problem (starting from when each problem statement was first seen/opened).
